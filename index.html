About Us

Locally owned & operated, Pakenham Music’s ‘Mission Statement’ is to inspire Musicians - both aspiring & experienced - by getting them into the RIGHT Instruments & Accessories.

Jake & Nick (proprietors) began their musical journeys around the age of 10, and soon after found themselves performing together in a ‘rock band’ at Berwick Primary School. They since haven’t stopped playing in bands & teaching music, and now pass on their collective experience to all those who frequent Pakenham Music.

The boys pride themselves on hand-picking the huge range of quality Instruments, Accessories & Music Books available In-Store & Online. New Stock arrives weekly so be sure to get on down for a look-see!
